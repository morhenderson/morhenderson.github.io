Filtering Algorithms

I first learned about Kalman filters in a graduate level course titled "Statistical Estimation for Dynamical Systems". Later I served as teaching assistant for the course and took an advanced section that dove deeper into the theory and included Bayesian, particle, and other filter methods.

Buffmo

As TA for the Statistical Estimation course, I decided to write a basic but thorough tutorial on the standard Kalman Filter (including truth modeling) applied to a simple linear dynamical system. It includes a Python program (Buffmo.py) and a comprehensive writeup (Buffmo.pdf) covering the mathematical formulation of the problem and detailed descriptions of the program.


Download Buffmo.py |  Download Buffmo.pdf

OD-Filters

After my semester as TA, I was impressed by the final projects students had submitted and decided to revisit my own project from the course with the benefit of a few years of practice. The theme of the project is statistical orbit determination for non-linear orbital dynamics, and includes an involved measurement model as well as linearized, extended, and unscented Kalman filters (LKF, EKF, & UKF).


Download OD_Filters.zip |  Download OD_Writeup.pdf
